research

My research falls under human-computer interaction, in particular, I volunteer with the ACM's Special Interest Group on Computer-Human Interaction, SIGCHI. I've served as committee and PC member of many SIGCHI conferences, including CHI, CSCW, UIST, UBICOMP, IUI among others.

I am currently named Co-Investigator (Co-I) on the PETRAS Project ReTIPS: Respectful Things in Private Spaces, and was previously a senior researcher in the EPSRC project SOCIAM: The Theory and Practice of Social Machines, under PI Sir Nigel Shadbolt. In 2011, I received my Ph.D. from the Haystack Group at MIT CSAIL, under Prof. David Karger.
