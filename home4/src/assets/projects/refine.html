Apps, devices, and services typically share data about users with multiple-sometimes dozens-of different companies and organisations for various purposes. Yet, people remain largely unaware of such data collection activities-and remain even less aware of how such practicies might put them at risk.

We feel that an important first step in helping people protect themselves online is to be able to understand how their data is being used, by whom, and what they can do about it. To this end, the X-Ray Project has worked on several tools and systems to make data collection and processing activities of apps, services and devices, knowable and actionable.
